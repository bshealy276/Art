About

Izabella
Shealy

Izabella Shealy is a self-taught painter whose work draws from memory and place — the particular quality of light in California, the open expanses of the Mountain West, and the quiet textures of everyday life.

Working in oil on canvas and wood panel, she moves between observation and feeling, returning often to landscapes, interiors, and figures that resist easy resolution. She lives and works in Brooklyn, New York.
